Hi, I'm Minji Kim!

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Statistics and Operations Research (STOR) at UNC Chapel Hill, under the guidance of Prof. Vladas Pipiras. My research focuses on leveraging multiple computer models with varying levels of fidelity (in terms of accuracy and cost) to achieve more accurate estimations, a field known as multi-fidelity (MF) modeling.

I've interned at LLNL in the Summer of 2022 and at MOLOCO in the Summer of 2023. More details are available in my CV.
