Hi, I'm Minji Kim!

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Statistics and Operations Research (STOR) at UNC Chapel Hill, under the supervision of Prof. Vladas Pipiras. My research focuses on leveraging multiple computer models with varying levels of fidelity (in terms of accuracy and cost) to achieve more accurate estimations, a field known as multi-fidelity modeling.

I hold a B.S. (2019) and M.S. (2021) in Statistics from Seoul National University, where I was mentored by Prof. Hee-Seok Oh. I've interned at LLNL in the Summer of 2022 and at Moloco in the Summer of 2023. More details are available in my CV.
